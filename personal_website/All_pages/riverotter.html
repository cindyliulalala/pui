Virtual Tour: River Otter Activity

During the pandemic, Carnegie Museum of Natural History (CMNH) provided virtual tours that allow families and students to engage with the exhibitions remotely.

Considering the challenges of tours hosted over Zoom, we developed an online interactive activity plan to enhance the virtual tour experience for grade schoolers. The activities include two digital games and one hands-on workshop distributed pre, during, and post the tour. The learning content and interactive features of the activities are designed to enhance students' active engagement and understanding of wildlife.
